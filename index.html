About me


I was born in Southern Italy, in a small village by the seaside.

I moved to the UK for uni and studied Computer Science at the University of Southampton (UK) and Artificial Intelligence at NUS in Singapore. I currently work in NLPNatural Language Processing research in London.

I started writing Fantasy at the age of 14, driven by the works of Tolkien, Rowling and Paolini.

Aside from writing, I enjoy latin dancing, mushroom picking, and playing the piano.

For thoughts, comments or queries, drop me a line here.

The Trickster
 

Aged 13 and living with his widowed mother, all Nero wants is an apprenticeship and to win the attention of Elva, a street illusionist. When Elva gets into trouble for tricking a member of the local mafia, the Hades, Nero saves her and asks her to show him the illusion. Everything changes when his mother falls very ill, and he has to find the means for obtaining her expensive medicine. Mentored by Elva’s father, Nero sets up a street performance that earns him both the money for the medicine and Elva’s long-sought attention. But his rising popularity brings him to the attention of the Hades, who will do anything in their power to crush him.
